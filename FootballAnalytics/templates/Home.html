HardlyBaflin Blog

Welcome to Hardly Baflin, the place where I attempt to present my observations, data-driven insights, and visualizations related to all things football. The purpose behind this dedicated space is to prove that football analytics can be hugely accessible and insightful to anybody interested. As football analytics has surged in prominence, it has paved the way for some very interesting data-rich narratives able to invigorate seasoned fans and provide newcomers with fresh perspectives on the game we love.

However, despite its growing prominence, football analytics still faces skepticism and uncertainty among certain fans, pundits and media circles. Former footballer and head coach of Tottenham Hotspur and Aston Villa, Tim Sherwood, openly dismisses the approach of utilizing football analytics to assist coaches in finding new strategies for improved match outcomes. “ The moto is if you can't convince, confuse. Transitions? Half spaces? What a load of nonsense. The guys that make decisions at Football Clubs are academics, they're getting baffled with Science. ”

Sherwood's views are undoubtedly echoed by some, and it is evident that a sense of uncertainty still surrounds many facets of football club performance, fueling this skepticism. However, rather than perpetuate this notion, we will instead follow the growing community that leverages a more scientific approach to shed light upon various football phenomena.
